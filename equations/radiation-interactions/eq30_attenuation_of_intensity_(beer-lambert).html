Equation 30: Attenuation of Intensity (Beer-Lambert)

Equation:

\[I(x) = I_0 e^{-\mu_t x}\]

Variables:

  • \(I(x)\): intensity at depth x
    • \n
  • \(I_0\): initial intensity
    • \n
  • \(\mu_t\): linear attenuation coefficient
    • \n
  • \(x\): thickness

Description:

Exponential attenuation of beam intensity by a material with linear attenuation coefficient \mu_t.
