Equation 36: Mass Attenuation Coefficient Composition

Equation:

\[\left(\frac{\mu_t}{\rho}\right)=\sum_i \left(\frac{\mu_i}{\rho}\right)\]

Variables:

  • \(\mu_t/\rho\): Total mass attenuation coefficient [cm2/g]
  • \(\mu_i/\rho\): Mass attenuation for each photon interaction type, i [cm2/g]

Description:

Mass attenuation coefficient is the sum of mass attenuation coefficient from each photon interaction type.
