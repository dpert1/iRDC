Equation 36: Mass Attenuation Coefficient Composition

Equation:

\[\left(\frac{\mu_t}{\rho}\right)=\sum_i \left(\frac{\mu_i}{\rho}\right)\]

Variables:

  • \(\mu_t/\rho\): mass attenuation coefficient of mixture
    • \n
  • \(\mu_i/\rho\): mass attenuation of component i

Description:

Total mass attenuation coefficient of a mixture is the sum of component mass attenuation coefficients (weighted appropriately).
