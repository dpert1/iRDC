Equation 39: Point Source Flux with Attenuation

Equation:

\[\phi(r) = \frac{S_p}{4\pi r^2} e^{-\Sigma_t r}\]

Variables:

  • \(\phi(r)\): flux at distance r
  • \(S_p\): source strength [particles/s]
  • \(r\): distance
  • \(\Sigma_t\): macroscopic total cross section

Description:

Flux from a point source decreases with inverse square law and exponential attenuation in the medium.
