Equation 34: Mass Attenuation to Linear Attenuation Relationship

Equation:

\[\mu_t = \left(\frac{\mu_t}{\rho}\right)\rho\]

Variables:

  • \(\mu_t\): Total linear attenuation coefficient [1/cm]
  • \(\mu_t/\rho\): Mass attenuation coefficient [cm2>/g]
  • \(\rho\): Mass density [g/cm$^3$]

Description:

Linear attenuation coefficient obtained from mass attenuation coefficient. Useful because mass attenuation coefficients are tabulated while linear attenuation coefficients may not be.
