Equation 35: Macroscopic Cross Section

Equation:

\[\Sigma_t = \Sigma_s + \Sigma_a\]

Variables:

  • \(\Sigma_t\): Total macroscopic cross section [1/cm]
  • \(\Sigma_s\): Macroscopic scattering cross section[1/cm]
  • \(\Sigma_a\): Macroscopic absorption cross section[1/cm]

Description:

Total macroscopic cross section is the sum of the two possible neutron interactions: scattering and absorption.
