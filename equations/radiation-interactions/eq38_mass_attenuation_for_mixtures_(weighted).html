Equation 38: Mass Attenuation for Mixtures (weighted)

Equation:

\[\left(\frac{\mu_i}{\rho}\right)=\sum_j w_j \left(\frac{\mu_i}{\rho}\right)_j\]

Variables:

  • \(\mu_i/\rho\): mass attenuation coefficient
    • \n
  • \(w_j\): weight fraction of component j
    • \n
  • \((\mu_i/\rho)_j\): component j mass attenuation

Description:

Composite mass attenuation coefficient formed from weight-fraction-weighted component contributions.
