Equation 37: Macroscopic Cross Section for Reaction i

Equation:

\[\Sigma_i = N \sigma_i(E) g_i(T)\]

Variables:

  • \(\Sigma_i\): macroscopic cross section for interaction i
    • \n
  • \(N\): number density
    • \n
  • \(\sigma_i(E)\): microscopic cross section [barns]
    • \n
  • \(g_i(T)\): temperature-dependent factor

Description:

Macroscopic cross section equals number density times microscopic cross section and any temperature-dependent correction.
