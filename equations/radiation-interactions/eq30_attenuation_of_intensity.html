Equation 30: Attenuation of Photon Intensity

Equation:

\[I(x) = I_0 e^{-\mu_t x}\]

Variables:

  • \(I(x)\): Intensity of photons at distance x [1/(cm2>-s)]
  • \(I_0\): Initial photon intensity [1/(cm2>-s)]
  • \(\mu_t\): Total linear attenuation coefficient [1/cm]
  • \(x\): Distance [cm]

Description:

Exponential attenuation of photon beam intensity by a material.
