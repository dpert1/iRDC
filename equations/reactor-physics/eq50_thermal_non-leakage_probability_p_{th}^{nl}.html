Equation 50: Thermal Non-leakage Probability P_{th}^{NL}

Equation:

\[P_{th}^{NL} = \frac{1}{1 + \dfrac{L^2 T B^2}{C}}\]

Variables:

  • \(P_{th}^{NL}\): thermal non-leakage probability
    • \n
  • \(L^2\): diffusion length squared
    • \n
  • \(T,B,C\): geometric/material parameters

Description:

Thermal non-leakage probability in diffusion approximation; decreases with larger buckling and material properties.
