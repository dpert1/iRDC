Equation 47: Six-Factor Formula for Criticality

Equation:

\[k_{eff} = \varepsilon\,p\,f\,\eta\,P_{NL}^{f}\,P_{NL}^{th}\]

Variables:

  • \(k_{eff}\): Effective multiplication factor [-]
  • \(\varepsilon\): Fast fission factor [-]
  • \(p\): Resonance escape probability [-]
  • \(f\): Thermal utilization factor [-]
  • \(\eta\): Thermal reproduction factor, or thermal fission factor [-]
  • \(P_{NL}^{f},P_{NL}^{th}\): Non-leakage probabilities for fast and thermal neutrons [-]

Description:

Six-factor formula for calculating the criticality of a nuclear reactor. Must be a thermal reactor where neutrons are slowed down to the thermal spectrum.
