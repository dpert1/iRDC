Equation 49: Fast Non-leakage Probability P$_{NL}^{f}$

Equation:

\[P_{NL}^{f} = e^{-B_C^2 \tau _T}\]

Variables:

  • \(P_{NL}^{f}\): Fast non-leakage probability [-]
  • \(B_C\): Core buckling [1/cm]
  • \(\tau _\): Fermi age, material property [cm$^2$]

Description:

Approximate form expressing the probability that fast neutrons do not leak from the core; depends on geometry and material properties.
