Equation 46: Reaction Rate \(R_i\)

Equation:

\[R_i = \Sigma_i \phi(E)\]

Variables:

  • \(R_i\): Reaction rate density for interaction i [reactions/(cm3-s)]
  • \(\Sigma_i\): Macroscopic cross section for interaction i [1/cm]
  • \(\phi(E)\): Neutron flux at energy E [1/(cm2-s)]

Description:

Reaction rate per unit volume obtained by multiplying macroscopic cross section by particle flux.
