Equation 52: Thermal Reproduction Factor η

Equation:

\[\eta = \nu \frac{\Sigma_f^F}{\Sigma_a^F}\]

Variables:

  • \(\eta\): Thermal reproduction factor, or thermal fission factor [-]
  • \(\nu\): Average neutrons per fission [-]
  • \(\Sigma_f^F\): Macroscopic fission cross section for fuel [1/cm]
  • \(\Sigma_a^F\): Macroscopic absorption cross section for fuel [1/cm]

Description:

Average number of neutrons produced per neutron absorbed in the fuel.
