Equation 45: Number of Collisions for Energy Change

Equation:

\[n = \frac{1}{\xi}\ln\frac{E_1}{E_2}\]

Variables:

  • \(n\): average number of collisions
    • \n
  • \(\xi\): log-energy decrement
    • \n
  • \(E_1,E_2\): initial and final energies

Description:
