Equation 40: Scattering Energy Transfer (lab frame)

Equation:

\[E' = \frac{1}{(A+1)^2}\Bigl(\sqrt{E}\cos\theta_s + \sqrt{E(A^2-1+\cos^2\theta_s)}\Bigr)^2\]

Variables:

  • \(E'\): scattered particle energy
  • \(E\): initial energy
  • \(A\): target mass ratio
  • \(\theta_s\): scattering angle

Description:

Energy of a scattered particle after collision with a nucleus of mass ratio A; derived from two-body kinematics.
