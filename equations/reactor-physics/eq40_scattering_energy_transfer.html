Equation 40: Scattering Energy Transfer (lab frame)

Equation:

\[E' = \frac{1}{(A+1)^2}\Bigl(\sqrt{E}\cos\theta_s + \sqrt{E(A^2-1+\cos^2\theta_s)}\Bigr)^2\]

Variables:

  • \(E'\): = Kinetic energy of scattered neutron [eV or MeV]
  • \(E\): Initial kinetic energy of neutron [eV or MeV]
  • \(A\): Target atomic mass number [-]
  • \(\theta_s\): Scattering angle [° or rad]

Description:

Energy of a scattered neutron after collision with a nucleus; derived from two-body kinematics.
