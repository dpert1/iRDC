Equation 48: Infinite Multiplication Factor k_$\infty$

Equation:

\[k_{\infty} = \varepsilon\,p\,f\,\eta\]

Variables:

  • \(k_{\infty}\): Infinite multiplication factor [-]
  • \(\varepsilon\): Fast fission factor [-]
  • \(p\): Resonance escape probability [-]
  • \(f\): Thermal utilization factor [-]
  • \(\eta\): Thermal reproduction factor, or thermal fission factor [-]

Description:

Four-factor formula for expressing the infinite multiplication factor of a reactor. That is, the multiplication factor if the reactor were infinitely large.
