Equation 49: Fast Non-leakage Probability P_f^{NL}

Equation:

\[P_f^{NL} = e^{-B^2 C \tau T}\]

Variables:

  • \(P_f^{NL}\): fast non-leakage probability
    • \n
  • \(B\): buckling
    • \n
  • \(C,\tau,T\): geometry/material dependent parameters

Description:

Approximate form expressing the probability that fast neutrons do not leak from the system; depends on geometry and material properties.
