Equation 6: Number Density from Weight Fraction

Equation:

\[N_i = \frac{w_i \rho_x N_A}{A_i}\]

Variables:

  • \(N_i\): Number density of isotope i [#/cm3]
  • \(w_i\): Weight fraction of isotope i [-]
  • \(\rho_x\): Mass density of element x [g/cm3]
  • \(N_A\): Avogadro's number [#/mol]
  • \(A_i\): Atomic mass of isotope i [g/mol]

Description:

Number density for isotope i calculated using weight fraction
