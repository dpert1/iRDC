Equation 1: Weighted Atomic Mass (Ax)

Equation:

\[A_x = \sum_i \gamma_i A_i\]

Variables:

  • \(A_x\): Weighted atomic mass of mixture
    • \n
  • \(\gamma_i\): Fractional contribution of component i
    • \n
  • \(A_i\): Atomic mass of component i

Description:

Weighted atomic mass of a mixture calculated as the sum of component atomic masses weighted by their fractions.
