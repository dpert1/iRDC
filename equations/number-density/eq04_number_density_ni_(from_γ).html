Equation 4: Number Density Ni (from γ)

Equation:

\[N_i = \frac{\gamma_i \rho_x N_A}{A_x}\]

Variables:

  • \(N_i\): Number density of species i [particles/cm^3]
    • \n
  • \(\gamma_i\): fractional abundance of i
    • \n
  • \(\rho_x\): mixture density [g/cm^3]
    • \n
  • \(N_A\): Avogadro's number
    • \n
  • \(A_x\): effective atomic mass

Description:

Number density (particles per unit volume) for component i computed from its fraction, density and Avogadro's number.
