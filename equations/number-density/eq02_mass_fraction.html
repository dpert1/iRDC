Equation 2: Weight Fraction Calculation

Equation:

\[w_x = \frac{n A_x}{n A_x + m A_y}\]

Variables:

  • \(w_x\): Weight fraction of element x [-]
  • \(n\): Number of x atoms in compound [-]
  • \(A_x\): Atomic mass of element x [g/mol or amu]
  • \(m\): Number of y atoms in compound [-]
  • \(A_y\): Atomic mass of element y [g/mol or amu]

Description:

Weight fraction of element x in a two-component compound.
