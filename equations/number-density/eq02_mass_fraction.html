Equation 2: Mass Fraction wx

Equation:

\[w_x = \frac{n A_x}{n A_x + m A_y}\]

Variables:

  • \(w_x\): Mass fraction of component x
  • \(n\): number of moles of x
  • \(A_x\): atomic mass of x
  • \(m\): number of moles of y
  • \(A_y\): atomic mass of y

Description:

Mass fraction of component x in a two-component mixture expressed using mole counts and atomic masses.
