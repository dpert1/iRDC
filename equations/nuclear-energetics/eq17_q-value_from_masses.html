Equation 17: Q-value from Masses

Equation:

\[Q = \left[\sum m_{0,i} - \sum m_{0,f} \right] c^2\]

Variables:

  • \(Q\): reaction Q-value [J or MeV]
  • \(\sum m_{0,i}\): sum of initial rest masses
  • \(\sum m_{0,f}\): sum of final rest masses
  • \(c\): speed of light

Description:

Q-value of a nuclear reaction computed from the difference in rest masses between initial and final states.
