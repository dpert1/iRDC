Equation 15: Neutron Separation Energy

Equation:

\[S_n(^{A}_{Z}X) = [M(^{A-1}_{Z}X) + m_n - M(^{A}_{Z}X)] c^2\]

Variables:

  • \(S_n\): Neutron separation energy [MeV]
  • \(M(^{A-1}_{Z}X)\): Mass of nuclide with one fewer neutron [amu]
  • \(m_n\): Mass of neutron [amu]
  • \(M(^{A}_{Z}X)\): Mass of original nuclide [amu]
  • \(c^2\): Mass-energy equivalence factor, 931.494013 [MeV/amu]
