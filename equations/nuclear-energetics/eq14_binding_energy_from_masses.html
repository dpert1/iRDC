Equation 14: Binding Energy from Masses

Equation:

\[BE(^{A}_{Z}X) = [Z M(^{1}H) + (A-Z) m_n - M(^{A}_{Z}X)] c^2\]

Variables:

  • \(BE(^{A}_{Z}X)\): Binding energy of nucleus ^{A}_{Z}X [MeV]
  • \(M(^{1}H)\): Mass of hydrogen-1 atom [amu]
  • \(m_n\): Mass of neutron [amu]
  • \(M(^{A}_{Z}X)\): Mass of element ^{A}_{Z}X [amu]
  • \(c\): Mass-energy equivalence factor, 931.5 [MeV/amu]

Description:

Binding energy computed from mass differences between constituent nucleons and the measured nuclear mass.
