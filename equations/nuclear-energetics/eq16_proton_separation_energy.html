Equation 16: Proton Separation Energy

Equation:

\[S_p(^{A}_{Z}Y) = [M(^{A-1}_{Z}X) + M(^{1}_{1}H) - M(^{A}_{Z}Y)] c^2\]

Variables:

  • \(S_p\): Proton separation energy [MeV]
  • \(M(^{A}_{Z}Y)\): Mass of nuclide with one fewer proton [amu]
  • \(M(^{1}_{1}H)\): Mass of hydrogen-1 [amu]
  • \(M(^{A-1}_{Z}X)\): Mass of original nuclide [amu]
  • \(c^2\): Mass-energy equivalence factor, 931.494013 [MeV/amu]
