Equation 19: General Two-body Q Relation

Equation:

\[Q = T_1\left(\frac{m_1}{m_4}-1\right)-\frac{2}{m_4}\sqrt{m_1 m_3 T_1 T_3}\cos\theta + T_3\left(\frac{m_3}{m_4}+1\right)\]

Variables:

  • \(Q\): Reaction Q-value [MeV]
  • \(T_1,T_3\): Kinetic energies of small particles [MeV]
  • \(m_1,m_3,m_4\): Masses of particles [amu]
  • \(\theta\): Scattering angle [°]

Description:

Kinematic relation for Q in a binary reaction including masses, kinetic energies and scattering angle. Implied nomenclature: 1+2 → 3+4. 1 and 3 are light particles; 2 and 4 are heavy particles. Particle 2 must be stationary.
