Equation 18: Q-value from Kinetic Energy Change

Equation:

\[Q = T_f - T_i\]

Variables:

  • \(Q\): reaction Q-value
    • \n
  • \(T_f\): total kinetic energy of products
    • \n
  • \(T_i\): total kinetic energy of reactants

Description:

Alternate definition of Q-value as the change in total kinetic energy between final and initial states.
