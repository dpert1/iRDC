Equation 13: Semi-empirical Mass (Weizsäcker) Formula

Equation:

\[E_B = a_v A - a_s A^{2/3} - a_c \frac{Z^2}{A^{1/3}} - a_a \frac{(A-2Z)^2}{A} \pm a_p A^{-1/2}\]

Variables:

  • \(E_B\): Binding energy [MeV]
  • \(a_v,a_s,a_c,a_a,a_p\): Weizsäcker coefficients (volume, surface, coulomb, asymmetry, parity)
  • \(A\): Atomic mass number [-]
  • \(Z\): Atomic number [-]

Description:

Semi-empirical formula estimating nuclear binding energy using five terms: volume, surface, Coulomb, asymmetry, and parity.
