Equation 28: Excitation Energy

Equation:

\[E^* = S_n + T_n\]

Variables:

  • \(E^*\): Excitation energy of compound nucleus [MeV]
  • \(S_n\): Neutron separation energy [MeV]
  • \(T_n\): Incident neutron kinetic energy [MeV]

Description:

Excitation energy of a compound nucleus after absorption of neutron. Used for comparing excitation energy to threshold energy for fission.
