Equation 28: Excitation Energy

Equation:

\[E^* = S_n + T_n\]

Variables:

  • \(E^*\): excitation energy
  • \(S_n\): neutron separation energy
  • \(T_n\): neutron kinetic energy

Description:

Excitation energy of a residual nucleus after neutron emission equals the separation energy plus neutron kinetic energy.
