Equation 26: Kinematic Threshold of Reaction

Equation:

\[E_{K1} \approx -\left(1+\frac{m_1}{m_2}\right)Q\]

Variables:

  • \(E_{K1}\): Kinematic Threshold energy of Particle 1 [MeV]
  • \(m_1,m_2\): Mass of particle 1 or 2 [amu]
  • \(Q\): Reaction Q-value [MeV]

Description:

An Expression for kinematic threshold energy for endoergic nuclear reaction. Reaction energy is provided by initial kinetic energy.
