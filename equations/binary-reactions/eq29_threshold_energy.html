Equation 29: Threshold Energy

Equation:

\[E_{1}^{th} = \max(E_1^K,\;E_1^C)\]

Variables:

  • \(E_{1}^{th}\): Threshold energy for reaction [MeV]
  • \(E_{1}^{K}\): Kinematic threshold energy [MeV]
  • \(E_{1}^{C}\): Coulombic barrier energy [MeV]

Description:

Reaction threshold energy is the greater of kinematic and Coulomb barrier thresholds. The energy must be supplied by the kinetic energy of the incident particle, 1.
