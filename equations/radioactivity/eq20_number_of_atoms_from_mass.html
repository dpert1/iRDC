Equation 20: Number of Atoms from Mass

Equation:

\[N = \frac{m N_A}{A}\]

Variables:

  • \(N\): number of atoms or nuclei
    • \n
  • \(m\): mass of sample [g]
    • \n
  • \(N_A\): Avogadro's number
    • \n
  • \(A\): atomic/mass number [g/mol]

Description:

Converts mass to number of atoms using Avogadro's number and molar mass.
