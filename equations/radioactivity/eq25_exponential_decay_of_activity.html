Equation 25: Exponential Decay of Activity

Equation:

\[A(t) = A_0 e^{-\lambda t}\]

Variables:

  • \(A(t)\): Activity at time t [Bq or Ci]
  • \(A_0\): Initial activity [Bq or Ci]
  • \(\lambda\): Decay constant [1/s]
  • \(t): Time [s]

Description:

Exponential decay of activity of radioactive species.
